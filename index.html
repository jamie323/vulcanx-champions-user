✦ THE JOURNEY ✦

How It Works

Four simple stages. Fifty levels. One legendary creature.

STEP 1
🥚

ADOPT

Choose your species and bloodline. Your whelp is minted as a one-of-a-kind NFT, bound forever to your wallet.

STEP 2
🧪

FEED

Earn potions from VulcanX quests. Feed them to raise stats — strength, vitality, agility, ferocity — and unlock evolutions.

STEP 3
⚔️

EVOLVE

Push through fifty levels across nine legendary stages. Bond with your champion as their codex unlocks — chapter by chapter.

STEP 4
✨

ASCEND

At the Legendary tier your champion awakens — transformed into a living, animated masterpiece. A one-of-a-kind animated NFT, bound to your wallet forever.
