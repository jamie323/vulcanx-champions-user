✦ The Apothecary ✦

All about potions

Potions are the food of your champion. Every bottle raises a specific trait and nudges the Evolution Bar forward. Understand these and you understand the game.

🧪 Trait potions — the six colors

Each trait has its own potion. They lift that stat by an amount that decays as you invest more into it at a stage — early pours are potent, later pours less so.

💪StrengthRaw damage output
❤️VitalityHealth & survivability
🏃AgilitySpeed & dodge
⚔️FerocityCritical power
🧠IntelligenceMagic & cunning
🍀LuckLoot & crit chance

🎚️ Three tiers — Minor, Major, Epic

Same trait, different potency. Stronger tiers drop rarely from chests.

TierStat gainBar fill (random)Drop rarity
Minor+5 stat2–6%~70% of chest rolls
Major+15 stat (×3)6–12%~25%
Epic+40 stat (×8)12–20%~5%

Heads-up: the stat gain is the same at every level — pacing comes from stat caps growing each stage. The bar-fill roll is random within the tier range, so two keepers fed the same potions won't land on the exact same %. Bloodline-aligned traits (e.g. STR for Ironfang) gain a touch more from their matching potion.

⚡ Evolution Potion

One-use. Consumed at a stage-gate level (L3 · 5 · 7 · 10 · 13 · 15 · 18 · 22 · 26 · 30 · 34 · 38 · 41 · 44 · 46 · 48 · 49 · 49 · 50) when your Evolution Bar is full. Triggers the transformation to the next stage — new silhouette, new cap tier, new floor stats.

✨ Blessing (40 points)

A flexible stat-allocation potion. When used you choose how to split 40 points across the six traits. Great for fixing a lopsided build or pushing a specific stat. Also fills 10-25% of the bar.

🔒 Species-lock

An Orc potion only feeds Orc champions. An Elf potion only feeds Elves. You can't cross-pollinate — keep them separate in your wallet.

🔥 What happens at the cap?

Every trait has a cap at each stage. When you try to feed a potion into a maxed trait, the potion is still consumed and still fills the bar — but the stat doesn't rise further. This means you can always evolve your way out of a capped state.

🧬 Where do potions come from?

In prod: potions are ERC-1155 NFTs that drop from treasure chests across the Vulcan Forged ecosystem (quests, Runner, Forge Arena) and can be bought/sold on the marketplace. During QA they're DB-backed — you got 80 free to test with.

Get started

Three minutes from arrival to your first hatched champion. No app downloads, no email — just your wallet.

  1. Connect your wallet. The wallet gate prompts MetaMask or WalletConnect. We'll switch you to Elysium (chain 1339) automatically if needed, then ask you to sign a short message to prove wallet ownership. Free — no gas.
  2. Get 80 free starter potions. First sign-in grants 10 of each trait Minor (STR/VIT/AGI/FER/INT/LCK), 10 Evolution Potions, and 10 Blessings. Enough to test the full 20-stage loop.
  3. Click "Adopt a Baby Champion". Pick a species (Orc only in QA), then a bloodline (Ironfang only), then name your champion. Names are permanent and shown on the NFT.
  4. Sign the adoption ceremony. Your wallet pops up to sign a "VulcanX:adopt:<name>" message. No PYR moves, no gas — purely proof you authorized the action.
  5. Egg cracks open. A 3-second cinematic plays as your Whelp emerges from the nest. You land in the champion view ready to feed, equip, and quest.
✏️
Don't love the name? Click the pencil icon next to your champion's name to rename. 5 PYR per rename in production; free in QA.

Feed your champion

Pouring potions raises stats and fills the Evolution Bar. Feed one at a time for precision, or batch-feed in bulk to skip the grind.

The six trait potions

💪

Strength

Raw physical damage. Drives melee and weapon swing power.

❤️

Vitality

Hit points and survivability. Tanky champions last longer in the arena.

🏃

Agility

Initiative + dodge. High AGI goes first in combat.

⚔️

Ferocity

Critical hit chance and crit damage. Spike potential.

🧠

Intelligence

Magic damage + spell resist. Primary for casters.

🍀

Luck

Proc chance for gear effects, dodge bonus, lucky drops.

Three feed modes

🥄

Single Tap

Click any trait bottle to feed exactly one potion. Best for fine-tuning a specific stat.

✨

Feed Me!

The big yellow button auto-picks the lowest-fill non-capped trait. Keeps your build balanced.

⏩

⏩ Batch

Pour 5 / 25 / 50 / All in one click. Auto-stops at the gate so you don't overshoot.

Two special potions

  • ⚡ Evolution Potion — consumed at a stage-gate level with a full Evolution Bar to advance to the next life stage.
  • ✨ Blessing — distribute 40 stat points freely across any traits. Also fills 10–25% of the Evolution Bar regardless of distribution.
⚠️
Trait at cap? Feeding into a maxed trait still consumes the potion and fills the bar — the stat just doesn't rise. You'll see "TRAIT MAXED · +X% BAR" float over the portrait.
📈
Diminishing returns: early pours into a trait give +5 stat each. As you invest more, gains drop to +3, then +1.5, then +1. Mid-game switching to Major (3×) and Epic (8×) tiers keeps progression brisk.

⚔️

Equipment system

Five gear slots, four rarity tiers, 24 species-locked weapons + cross-species accessories. Equipment boosts your Power Score and unlocks signature combat effects.

The five slots

⚔️

Weapon

Species-locked. Each species has 3 weapons with unique signature effects (Stunning Crit, Twin Strike, Bulwark, etc.)

🛡

Armor

Cross-species. Light Plate (dodge), Battle Mail (balanced), Heavy Cuirass (damage reduction).

💍

Trinket

Power Ring (random rolled stat) or Lucky Charm (+15% LCK + global proc bonus).

👢

Boots

Swift Boots: +AGI and an initiative bonus that almost always wins ties.

✨

Aura

+5% all stats (Common) up to +20% all stats (Legendary). Endgame flex slot.

How to equip

  1. Open your champion view. Find the Equipment card in the left column with 5 slot squares.
  2. Click any slot. A picker modal opens showing only items that fit (species-locked weapons; cross-species for the rest).
  3. Tap an item to equip. Power Score updates immediately. The slot border tints to its rarity color, with a +N power chip showing the gear's contribution.
  4. "View Inventory" below the slots opens your full owned set — sortable by rarity, grouped by slot.
🎒
Starter set: on first boot you get 9 mock items (3 weapons matching your species, 2 armor, 2 trinkets, boots, aura). Real NFT-backed equipment + chest drops from the Vulcan Forged ecosystem launch in production.

The Arena

Async stat battles vs bots. Pick your stance and ability, watch the animated reveal, climb the ELO ladder. Real player-vs-player coming in a later phase.

The four-step battle flow

  1. Click ⚔️ Arena in the top nav. Your active champion auto-loads with their equipped weapon and current ELO score.
  2. Pick your stance: ⚔️ Aggressive (+15% damage, -15% defense), ⚖️ Balanced (no modifiers), or 🛡️ Defensive (-15% damage, +15% defense).
  3. Pick your ability. Your bloodline signature is locked + automatic; you also pick 1 from a pool of 8 universal abilities (see below).
  4. Click "Find Opponent + Fight". The server generates a bot of your bracket, runs a deterministic 8-round battle, and streams the result back as an animated reveal — portraits shake on hits, damage popups fly, an event ticker shows the play-by-play.

Eight universal abilities

💥

Power Strike

First two hits this match deal +30% damage.

🛡️

Iron Skin

Take 25% less damage in rounds 1 and 2.

🚩

Rally

+10% damage per crit landed (caps at +30%).

🩸

Drain Touch

Heal 12% of damage dealt for the first 3 rounds.

💨

Quickstep

+20% dodge for the first 2 rounds.

🎯

Hunter's Mark

Your first crit deals +50% damage.

🪨

Stone Wall

First 2 hits taken deal 50% damage (then expires).

🩹

Pact of Blood

Pay 15% HP at start; gain +25% damage all match.

Four brackets

  • 🥚 Whelpling Cup — stages 0-4 (L1-L10) · starting ELO 100
  • 🐺 Adolescent — stages 5-9 (L13-L26) · starting ELO 200
  • 🛡️ Champion — stages 10-14 (L30-L44) · starting ELO 400
  • 👑 Legendary — stages 15-19 (L46-L50) · starting ELO 800
🤖
Bots match your fill ratio. A fresh L1 player faces a fresh L1 bot — no more 1-shot defeats. Bot equipment rarity scales with bracket: Whelpling matches mostly Common, Legendary fights see real Legendary gear.

Evolve through 20 life stages

Your champion passes through twenty distinct stages — not just age, but armor, scars, weapons, backdrop. Each stage looks visibly different.

Whelp→ Hatchling→ Pup→ Cub→ Youngling→ Apprentice→ Initiate→ Novice→ Warrior→ Veteran→ Champion→ Elite→ Hero→ Commander→ Warlord→ Exemplar→ Paragon→ Ascendant→ Mythic→ Legendary

Three things must align to evolve

  1. Hit a stage-gate level. Gates: L3, 5, 7, 10, 13, 15, 18, 22, 26, 30, 34, 38, 41, 44, 46, 48, 49, 49, 50.
  2. Fill the Evolution Bar to 100%. Feed potions or send out on quests until full.
  3. Have an Evolution Potion. Each evolution consumes one. You start with 10.

When all three line up, both the big Evolve button and the Evolution Potion card light up gold and pulse. Click either to trigger the cinematic — bass thunder, gold spark fountain, screen shake, the new stage art crossfading in. Stats carry over to the new stage's floor; the next cap opens.

🏆 Legendary — the prize

Reach stage 20 at Level 50 and your champion ascends to Legendary. In production this becomes an animated on-chain NFT — a fully realised masterpiece, bound forever to your wallet. Each Legendary is 1-of-1; no two keepers will arrive at the same stat distribution, name, equipped gear, and art progression path.

📊
Long road by design: roughly 1,700 Minor potions to reach Legendary on a greedy run. Major (3×) and Epic (8×) tiers cut that significantly. This is the long-term collectible economy — don't expect to Legendary in one QA session.

Feeding & potions

Out of potions?

Buy more in the 🛒 Shop (top bar) — Minors 5 PYR, Majors 15 PYR, Epics 40 PYR. Dummy TX in QA, no PYR deducted. In prod, potions drop from chests across the Vulcan Forged ecosystem.

Which trait will Feed Me fire?

The label under the button shows a preview, e.g. 🍀 next · 13 ready. Priority: non-capped traits with the lowest fill ratio first (keeps your build balanced).

Trait already maxed?

Feeding still consumes the potion and fills the Evolution Bar — the stat just doesn't rise. You'll see "TRAIT MAXED · +X% BAR" float over the portrait.

Same potions, different bar %?

Bar fill is a random roll inside each tier range (Minor 2–6%, Major 6–12%, Epic 12–20%). Lucky pours fill more. By design.

Different potion counts on two champs?

Potions are shared per wallet + per species. If counts differ, you're on two different wallets (likely the "fresh wallet" dev button).

Potions to reach Legendary?

Each Minor adds +5 stat, Major +15, Epic +40 — same at every level. Mixing tiers, expect a few thousand potions across all six stats to fill from L1 to Legendary. Long-term collectible economy by design.

Arena

Why do I keep losing?

Bots now scale to your stat fill ratio (Phase 5.7 fix). If you're losing 5+ in a row, try a different stance or universal ability — Iron Skin and Stone Wall help under-statted champions punch up.

Where does my ELO live?

localStorage for now (vs-bots MVP). Real PvP + persistent leaderboard arrive in Phase 8.

Can I rematch the same bot?

No — each match generates a fresh bot from your bracket. The seed is logged so disputes can be replayed exactly.

Do equipment + ability stack?

Yes. Weapon rarity multiplies damage, the universal ability fires its trigger, and your bloodline signature passive runs all match.

Quests & Daily

Can I close the tab during a quest?

Yes — quests run server-side. Come back any time after the timer ends and click ⚡ Claim Rewards.

Can multiple champs quest at once?

Yes. Each champion runs its own quest independently. There's no party limit.

Missed a day — does my streak reset?

Yes. Skip even one calendar day and you go back to Day 1. Day 7 is the big bundle, so a full week matters.

Can I rename my champion?

Yes — click the ✏️ next to the name. 5 PYR per rename in prod, free in QA (dummy TX).
