✦ THE GAME ✦

How to raise your Champion

Adopt a baby creature. Feed it potions you earn across the Vulcan Forged ecosystem. Evolve it through 20 life stages. Keep its NFT in sync with every step. One-of-a-kind, forever yours.

1
Step One

Adopt a Champion

Pick your species — Orc, Elf, Goblin, Hellknight, Drakkin, Dwarf, Beastkin, or Wraith — then choose a bloodline from six variants.

Adoption costs 10 PYR. In return you receive a one-of-a-kind baby champion, minted as an NFT directly to your wallet. Nobody else in the realm will ever have the same one.

2
Step Two

Quest for Chests & Potions

Potions are the food your champion needs to grow. You earn them by playing across the Vulcan Forged ecosystem:

  • Quests in VulcanVerse — dungeon runs, daily tasks, seasonal missions
  • Vulcan Runner — distance milestones and campaign stages
  • Forge Arena — ranked matches and tournaments
  • Events, airdrops, and limited-time chest drops

Each completed quest earns you a treasure chest. Chests open inside their own parts of the ecosystem — when they crack, potion NFTs land directly in your wallet. You don't need to come back here to open them.

💡 Don't have time to quest? Potions are tradeable NFTs — buy or sell them on the marketplace.

3
Step Three

Feed Your Champion

Open your champion's page — we check your wallet automatically and show every potion you own. Tap a bottle to feed it. The potion NFT is burned (destroyed forever), and your champion's matching stat rises while the Evolution Bar fills.

Potions come in three tiers — Minor (common, small boost), Major (uncommon, bigger), Epic (rare, massive). Every potion lifts one of six traits:

  • 💪StrengthRaw damage output
  • ❤️VitalityHealth & survivability
  • 🏃AgilitySpeed & dodge
  • ⚔️FerocityCritical power
  • 🧠IntelligenceMagic & cunning
  • 🍀LuckLoot & crit chance

Species lock: an Orc potion only feeds Orc champions. No Elf ever drinks Orc brew. Keep that in mind when farming chests for a specific pet.

Blessings & Evolution Potions are special drops: a Blessing gives you 40 stat points to distribute freely, and an Evolution Potion is what you'll need for the next step.

4
Step Four

Evolve & Sync to Chain

Your champion passes through 20 stages as it grows — not just age, but armor, scars, equipment, and raw power. Each stage looks visibly different. At certain level gates, feeding its Evolution Bar to 100% and spending one Evolution Potion triggers a transformation.

Here's the important bit: when your champion evolves, your wallet will prompt you to sync the new look to chain. Sign the transaction, and the NFT in your wallet updates to match the latest stage. Skip it and your NFT stays at the previous form — no cheating, no duplicates. You'll also be blocked from feeding more potions until you sync up.

✨ Your NFT always reflects the current version of your champion. Every evolution is committed on-chain. No one else will ever have your exact creature at your exact stage.

5
Step Five

Ascend to Legendary

Reach the final stage at Level 50, and your champion unlocks the Legendary tier — a fully animated one-of-one NFT, moving art bound forever to your wallet.

This is the prize. An animated, on-chain, truly unique champion raised entirely by you. Share it, showcase it, trade it, pass it down.
