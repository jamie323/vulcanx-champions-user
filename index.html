✦ The Apothecary ✦

All about potions

Potions are the food of your champion. Every bottle raises a specific trait and nudges the Evolution Bar forward. Understand these and you understand the game.

🧪 Trait potions — the six colors

Each trait has its own potion. They lift that stat by an amount that decays as you invest more into it at a stage — early pours are potent, later pours less so.

💪StrengthRaw damage output
❤️VitalityHealth & survivability
🏃AgilitySpeed & dodge
⚔️FerocityCritical power
🧠IntelligenceMagic & cunning
🍀LuckLoot & crit chance

🎚️ Three tiers — Minor, Major, Epic

Same trait, different potency. Stronger tiers drop rarely from chests.

TierEarly stat gainBar fillDrop rarity
Minor+5 stat2-6%~70% of chest rolls
Major+15 stat (×3)6-12%~25%
Epic+40 stat (×8)12-20%~5%

⚡ Evolution Potion

One-use. Consumed at a stage-gate level (L3 · 5 · 7 · 10 · 13 · 15 · 18 · 22 · 26 · 30 · 34 · 38 · 41 · 44 · 46 · 48 · 49 · 49 · 50) when your Evolution Bar is full. Triggers the transformation to the next stage — new silhouette, new cap tier, new floor stats.

✨ Blessing (40 points)

A flexible stat-allocation potion. When used you choose how to split 40 points across the six traits. Great for fixing a lopsided build or pushing a specific stat. Also fills 10-25% of the bar.

🔒 Species-lock

An Orc potion only feeds Orc champions. An Elf potion only feeds Elves. You can't cross-pollinate — keep them separate in your wallet.

🔥 What happens at the cap?

Every trait has a cap at each stage. When you try to feed a potion into a maxed trait, the potion is still consumed and still fills the bar — but the stat doesn't rise further. This means you can always evolve your way out of a capped state.

🧬 Where do potions come from?

In prod: potions are ERC-1155 NFTs that drop from treasure chests across the Vulcan Forged ecosystem (quests, Runner, Forge Arena) and can be bought/sold on the marketplace. During QA they're DB-backed — you got 80 free to test with.

How it Works

Adopt a baby creature · Feed it potions · Raise it through 20 life stages to Legendary.

The loop, in 60 seconds

VulcanX Champions is a creature-raising game. You adopt a baby creature, feed it potions to grow its stats, and evolve it through twenty distinct life stages to Legendary.

STEP 1
🔐

Connect

Sign in with your wallet on Elysium. Get 80 free starter potions.

STEP 2
🥚

Adopt

Pick a bloodline, name your champion, sign the mint TX.

STEP 3
🧪

Feed

Pour potions to raise stats. The Evolution Bar fills with each feed.

STEP 4
⚡

Evolve

At each level gate, burn an Evolution Potion to transform.

QA build: Only the Orc Ironfang bloodline is adoptable. You get 80 free starter potions on first sign-in. Transactions are signed but no PYR is deducted.

1. Connect your Wallet

When you first visit, you'll see a Connect Wallet screen. Tap it — your wallet (MetaMask or similar) will prompt you to:

  • Share your account so the app knows who you are.
  • Switch to Elysium (chainId 1339) — added automatically if missing.
  • Sign a short sign-in message to prove wallet ownership. This is free — no gas.

You'll be granted 80 starter potions

On your first sign-in: 10 of each trait potion (Strength, Vitality, Agility, Ferocity, Intelligence, Luck) in Minor tier, plus 10 Evolution potions and 10 Blessings. Enough to test the full 20-stage loop.

💡 Your progress persists. Come back anytime, sign in with the same wallet, and your champions and potions are right where you left them.

2. Adopt your Champion

Hit Adopt New in the top nav. You'll see all eight fantasy species, but only Orc is unlockable during QA — and within Orc, only the Ironfang bloodline is ready to hatch.

  • Other species and bloodlines are greyed out as "Coming soon".
  • Name your champion — the name is permanent and shown on its NFT.
  • Hit Adopt. Your wallet pops up for the dummy 10 PYR receipt TX.
  • Value is zero during QA — only a small Elysium gas fee.
⚠️ QA note: You must sign the TX in your wallet to complete adoption. If you cancel, the champion isn't created.

3. Feed your Champion

Open a champion from the Stable. You'll see portrait, level, evolution bar, stats, and your potion inventory in the left panel.

Tap any potion bottle. Your champion's matching stat rises (with diminishing returns — early pours into a trait are more potent than the 10th), and the Evolution Bar fills by 2-20%.

Six trait potions

💪StrengthRaw damage output
❤️VitalityHealth & survivability
🏃AgilitySpeed & dodge
⚔️FerocityCritical power
🧠IntelligenceMagic & cunning
🍀LuckLoot & crit chance

Two special potions

  • ⚡ Evolution Potion — consumed at a stage-gate level with a full bar to advance to the next life stage.
  • ✨ Blessing — grants 40 stat points you distribute freely across any traits. Also fills 10-25% of the bar.
🧪 During QA: every potion is a DB-backed record. After launch, potions become on-chain ERC-1155 NFTs earned from Vulcan Forged quests.

4. Evolve through 20 Life Stages

Your champion passes through 20 distinct life stages — not just age, but armor, scars, equipment, backdrop, and raw power. Each stage looks visibly different.

Whelp→ Hatchling→ Pup→ Cub→ Youngling→ Apprentice→ Initiate→ Novice→ Warrior→ Veteran→ Champion→ Elite→ Hero→ Commander→ Warlord→ Exemplar→ Paragon→ Ascendant→ Mythic→ Legendary

How evolution works

  1. Fill the Evolution Bar to 100% by feeding potions.
  2. Hit a stage-gate level (L3, 5, 7, 10, 13, 15, 18, 22, 26, 30, 34, 38, 41, 44, 46, 48, 49, 49, 50).
  3. Spend 1 Evolution Potion.
  4. Hit the Evolve button — your champion transforms.
  5. Stats carry up to the new stage's floor; the next cap opens.
🐛 QA testers: flag any stage where evolution seems stuck, stats drop on evolve, or the new stage art doesn't load.

5. Ascend to Legendary

Reach stage 20 at Level 50 and your champion unlocks the Legendary tier — a fully realised masterpiece, bound forever to your wallet.

In prod this becomes an animated on-chain NFT. This is the prize: a truly unique champion, raised entirely by you. Share it, showcase it, trade it, pass it down.

🏆 Each Legendary is 1-of-1 — no two keepers will arrive at the same stat distribution, name, and art progression path.

QA Notes & FAQ

What's real vs dummy in QA?

  • Wallet auth: real. SIWE on Elysium (chain 1339).
  • Adoption TX: real transaction, zero value. Tagged on-chain as VulcanX:adopt.
  • Potions: DB-backed for QA. ERC-1155 after launch.
  • Champions: DB-backed for QA. ERC-721 after launch.
  • Art: final for Orc Ironfang (20 stages). Other bloodlines coming.

What happens if I run out of potions?

In prod you earn more from chests in the wider Vulcan Forged ecosystem. In QA we can top you up via the admin tools — ping the Discord channel.

What if a trait is maxed?

Feeding a potion into a maxed trait still consumes the potion and fills the bar — the stat just doesn't rise. This keeps you progressing even if you over-invested early.

Can I trade/sell my champion?

Not in QA. In prod, yes — champions and potions both live on-chain and move through the Vulcan marketplace.
